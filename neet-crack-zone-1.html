Strategy

CRACK NEET SMARTER

Proven strategies followed by every NEET topper. No fluff — just what works.

01
NCERT is Your Bible

70% of NEET questions come directly from NCERT. Read every line, every diagram caption, every example. Don't skip a single word.

02
Solve PYQs Daily

Previous Year Questions reveal patterns. Solve at least 30 PYQs every day. You'll notice the same concepts recycled with new wording.

03
Revision Over New Topics

After your first pass, spend 60% of time revising. What you revise, you retain. What you forget, you lose marks on. Revise ruthlessly.

04
Mock Tests = Real Practice

Take a full mock test every week under exam conditions. Analyse your mistakes — they are your biggest teachers. Never skip the analysis.

05
Bio Gets You the Rank

Biology is 360 marks. A score of 330+ in Biology alone can secure your seat. Make it your strongest subject, not an afterthought.

06
Protect Your Sleep

A rested brain retains more in 4 hours than an exhausted brain in 10. Sleep 7–8 hours. Your memory consolidates during sleep — use it.
